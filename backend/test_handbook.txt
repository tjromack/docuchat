Company Employee Handbook

Welcome to TechCorp Solutions!

This handbook contains important information about our company policies, procedures, and benefits.

Remote Work Policy:
- Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week
- Core hours are 10 AM - 3 PM EST for team collaboration
- All remote workers must have reliable internet and a dedicated workspace

Vacation Policy:
- New employees receive 15 days PTO annually
- After 3 years, employees receive 20 days PTO annually  
- Vacation requests must be submitted 2 weeks in advance

Health Benefits:
- Company covers 80% of health insurance premiums
- Dental and vision coverage available
-  annual wellness stipend
